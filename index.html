Our Duo

Celeste and François, circus performers with two very diverse backgrounds, started working together in 2014 at the Quebec Circus School. They believe that as trapeze artists it's important to balance technique and artistry. They place equal importance on heart-stopping drops as they do awe-inspiring movement, all with the goal of taking your breath away.

Watch Trapeze Demo

Celeste

Celeste Bliss is a native Texan who has had a passion for movement and heights all her life. She studied belly dance, ballet, modern, jazz, and tap until she discovered circus at age 14. She has studied the Professional Track program at both the New England Center for Circus Arts and the Quebec Circus School. Her other disciplines include rope, static trapeze and any other apparatus that allows her to dance in the air.

Watch Celeste

Francois

Born in France and raised in Quebec, Francois grew up playing soccer, track and field, basketball, swimming and - most notably - volleyball at the collegiate level. Inspired by a circus cabaret in 2013, he decided to explore his athletic prospects in a more artistic environment. This led him to the Quebec Circus School, where he met Celeste. Along with being a trapeze artist François does aerial straps and plays the guitar.
